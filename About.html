About Me



I am a veteran and former All-Source Intelligence Analyst with the US Army, acheiving the rank of Staff Sergeant. I deployed twice to Afghanistan with the 5th Stryker Brigade and 2nd Stryker Brigade. I've served in nearly every capacity that an analyst can, including: current operations, targeting, collection management, open-source research, briefing and debriefing, technical writing, mission planning, instructing, and personnel management.


I applied for the Microsoft Software and Systems Academy (formerly Software Engineering Academy) and held the role of class leader and peer tutor. I hoped to at least gain additional education, if not a chance to work for Microsoft. I plan to take my technical skills and passion for learning to the private sector, where I hope to continue to grow as a person and a professional.
